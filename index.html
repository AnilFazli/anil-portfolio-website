About

Anil Fazli, who lives in Groningen and was born in Afghanistan, is a young and passionate person. In 2011, he started his studies at the Friesland College, for which he obtained his diploma in 2015. During his specialization he did an internship at 123Fris, an entrepreneurial company where he did market research, wrote daily publications and maintained relationships with clients as a junior account manager. To further expand his skills, his next internship was at Paradigm; one of the largest music festival and event organizers in the Netherlands, expanding his marketing knowledge internationally.
